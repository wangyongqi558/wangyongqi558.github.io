### 1. Abstract
We propose a Multi-modal Prompting method to adapt CLIP for Open-VidVRD by tuning both visual and textual prompts.

### 2. Methodology

![Methodology](/images/aaai24_method.png)
* **Dual-side Prompting**: Simultaneous optimization of visual and textual tokens.
* **Spatial-Temporal Context**: Capturing dynamic interactions through conditional prompts.

### 3. Key Results
Significant improvement on VidVRD dataset for unseen categories.
